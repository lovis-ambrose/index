Land Surveying

We do a detailed study and inspection by gathering information through observations, measurements in the field, Questionnaires or research of legal instruments and data analysis in the support of planning, designing and establishing of property boundaries. We establish land maps and boundaries for ownership, locations, area verification and other Governmentally required or civil law purposes.

Land Development and Subdivision

We do land Subdivision for housing and commercial purposes. It is our obligation to do mapping and measure the land accurately and estimate costs associated with infrastructure development, site preparation, and utilities installation.
